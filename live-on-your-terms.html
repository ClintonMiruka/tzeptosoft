How to build it:

  • Define Your Values: Truth, discipline, courage. Write 3–5 core principles. Musashi’s “Accept everything just the way it is” kept him unshaken. Your values are your spine.
  • Set Daily Rules: Wake at 4 a.m. Code 3 hours. Lift weights. No X after 8 p.m. A 2024 productivity study found structured routines boost output by 45%. Rules are freedom.
