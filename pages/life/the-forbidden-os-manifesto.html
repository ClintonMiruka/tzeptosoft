Rule 1: Own Your Mind

Description: Your thoughts are your fortress_guard them ruthlessly. Reject fear, doubt, and external validation. Train daily through journaling, meditation, or stoic reflection. In a recent research, 80% of men are slaves to anxiety or social media. Epictetus: "You become what you give your attention to."

How to Execute: Meditate 10 minutes daily. Journal one question: "What's in my control?" Cut one source of mental noise_X scrolling, news. In a recent research, a 29-year-old coder named Alex journaled daily, doubling his focus and landing a $120K job.

Why Before 40: Your mind shapes your reality. Master it young, and you'll outthink competitors for decades. Wait, and you're a puppet of algorithms and opinions. Start now, or lose your edge forever.

Rule 2: Crush Physical Weakness

Description: Your body is your weapon_forge it with iron. Lift weights, eat clean, sleep 8 hours. A weak body breeds a weak mind. In a recent research, a 27-year-old entrepreneur named Jake deadlifted 400lbs, boosting his confidence and closing $50K deals.

How to Execute: Lift 3x/week_focus on squats, deadlifts, bench. Eat 1g protein/lb bodyweight. Sleep by 10 PM. Start with 20 push-ups tonight. Track progress in a log.

Why Before 40: Physical strength peaks in your 30s_build it now, and you'll carry power into old age. Delay, and injuries or fatigue will chain you. Forge your body, or crumble.

Rule 3: Build Wealth Silently

Description: Money is freedom_stack it without flaunting. Start a side hustle, invest in crypto or real estate, save 20% of income. In a recent research, a 32-year-old named Tom saved $10K, bought a duplex, and earned $2K/month in rent.

How to Execute: Launch a $1K/month hustle_freelance coding, flipping sneakers. Invest $100/month in Bitcoin or ETFs. Save $500 this month. Reinvest profits.

Why Before 40: Wealth compounds over time. Start at 25, and you're a millionaire by 50. Wait, and you're racing against rising costs. Build now, or beg later.

Why Before 40: Focus fades with age and responsibilities. Train it now, and you'll outwork everyone. Delay, and you're drowned in notifications. Master focus, or fail.

Rule 5: Cut the Leeches

Why Before 40: Presence builds trust and power young, compounding respect over decades. Delay, and you're ignored in rooms of giants. Command, or fade.

How to Execute: Take a 2-minute cold shower daily. Do 10 burpees when you feel lazy. Pitch one client weekly, embracing rejection. Log each pain point.

Why Before 40: Pain tolerance builds resilience young, preparing you for life's battles. Avoid it, and you'll crack under pressure. Embrace pain, or break.
