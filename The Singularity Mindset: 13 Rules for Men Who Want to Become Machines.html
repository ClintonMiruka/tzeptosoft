Rule 01: Optimize Sleep Like Code

Sleep is not weakness; it is system recalibration. Neuroscience proves 7-8 hours of REM sleep amplifies cognitive throughput by 40%, consolidating neural connections. Less, and your processing lags. More, and you squander cycles. Schedule sleep like a kernel process: rigid, unyielding. Blue-light exposure post-20:00 disrupts melatonin by 22%; eliminate it with filters. In cyberpunk, sleep is the dock where data fuses into insight. In war, rested soldiers outlast the weary. Optimize or crash.

  • Sleep 7-8 hours, 22:00-06:00, no exceptions.
  • Deploy f.lux or blue-light glasses post-18:00.
  • Track REM with a wearable; target 4-5 90-minute cycles.
  • Use blackout curtains to simulate void conditions.
  • Consume 3mg melatonin 30 minutes pre-sleep if latency exceeds 15 minutes.
  • Keep bedroom at 18°C to maximize deep sleep efficiency.
  • Eliminate caffeine 8 hours before sleep to prevent adenosine interference.
  • Log sleep metrics daily to detect deviations.
  • Simulate dawn with a sunrise alarm for cortisol alignment.
  • Study sleep stage data weekly to refine protocol.
