Rule 1: Always Build a Loyal Army (Hire Top Talent)

Philosophy: A mafia thrives on loyalty. Your agency is only as strong as your crew—hire coders, designers, and managers who’d take a bullet for you. In Kenya, 1.2 million developers compete for jobs (GitHub, 2025), but only 10% are elite. Find them, train them, keep them. Loyalty over cheap labor.

Tactical Steps:

  • Recruit via Moringa School or Andela’s talent pool. Test candidates with real projects (e.g., build a CRUD app in 48 hours).
  • Offer equity or profit-sharing to lock in top talent. Pay 20% above market for A-players.
  • Build a culture of loyalty—weekly team dinners, transparent goals. Read Leaders Eat Last by Simon Sinek (1 chapter/day).
  • Join Tzeptosoft’s Telegram. Share one hiring win.

Punchline: A disloyal crew betrays. A loyal army conquers.

Rule 2: Never Show Your Hand (Work in Silence)

Philosophy: Loose lips sink ships. In Kenya’s cutthroat tech scene, competitors steal ideas, and clients poach talent. Operate like a shadow—reveal nothing until the deal’s done. 70% of failed startups leak strategies early (CB Insights, 2025).

Tactical Steps:

  • Use NDAs for all clients and staff. Enforce them ruthlessly.
  • Avoid social media flexing. Share wins only after contracts are signed.
  • Read The 48 Laws of Power by Robert Greene (1 law/day). Apply Law 4: Always Say Less Than Necessary.
  • Join Tzeptosoft’s Discord. Share one silent move.

Punchline: Silence is power. Noise is weakness.

Rule 3: The Product Is King (Only Deliver Excellence)

Philosophy: Mediocrity gets you whacked. Your software—apps, platforms, APIs—must be flawless. 80% of clients ditch agencies for poor quality (Gartner, 2025). Excellence builds your legend.

  • Use TDD (Test-Driven Development). Aim for 90% test coverage.
